About the Founder

Buying Time NYC was built from the heart of hustle — the spirit that turns vision into legacy. Founded by Peter Crocitto, a one-handed drummer and creative from New York, the brand stands for resilience, authenticity, and the art of turning pain into power. Alongside his loyal husky Chance, Peter continues to redefine what it means to build time into something timeless — where every struggle becomes fuel for creation.
