Petey is the founder of Buying Time NYC — a vision born from adversity, hustle, and pure creative fire. After a life-changing injury that cost him his arm, he refused to let that stop him. Instead, he became a one-handed drummer, a collector, a builder of brands, and a living testament that time is the most valuable currency.

Buying Time NYC represents second chances, relentless ambition, and a movement to turn pain into purpose. With Chance the husky by his side and a powerful partner in Shannon, Petey is carving out a lane where hustle meets healing — and where inspiration can be worn, watched, collected, or heard.
